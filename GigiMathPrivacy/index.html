Privacy Policy for Gigi Math

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Gigi Math is designed as an educational tool for children. We take your privacy and your child's safety seriously.

1. Data Collection: Gigi Math does NOT collect any personal information, location data, or contact details. All game progress, stars earned, and items purchased in the virtual store (hats, pets, glasses) are stored locally on the device or within secure, non-identifiable app data.

2. Authentication: Our login system is designed for user convenience. We do not share login credentials with third parties.

3. Advertisements and In-App Purchases: Gigi Math is a free app. It contains no third-party advertisements and no real-money in-app purchases.

4. Children’s Privacy: We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Contact: For any questions, contact us at grisel135@hotmail.com.
